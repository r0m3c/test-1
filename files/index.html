What the Club is About

We are a student let club organiztion that believes in helping Computer Science students succeed in the STEM field. We offer events that are aimed and designed to grow your understanding of Tech related soft and hard skills.

Whether you are aiming to be a Software Engineer, Data Scientist, or Project manager, we offer a community of like-minded individuals who are eager to connect and learn with others.

Events

Interested in learning Python, CSS/HTML/JS, or Swift? Then we are the club for you! We offer workshops for each of these programming languages and so much more, including Networking and Technical interview prep workshops. You can be a complete beginner or and advanced programmer to join us. Come and join any of our events to get started on your first coding project.

Resources

Are you interested in learning a new programming language or new framework? We offer a bunch of resources that we gathered and believe are best suited to help you out in this endeavor. Resources include information on Computer Science Classes at John Jay, learning Git/Github, getting started on learning a new language, and much more.

Collaborate

Here at the Computer Society Club, we want to help out as many students as we can. For that reason, we’ve decided to open up Assistant Executive positions on our board, to get fellow members more involved with the club and hopefully one day, into the Executive position. Come and checkout our open positions and apply.
